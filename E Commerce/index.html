3 Tips for Keeping Your Indoor Plants Healthy

  • Provide Adequate Light: Place your indoor plants in areas with sufficient natural light or supplement with artificial grow lights.
  • Water Properly: Avoid overwatering or underwatering by checking the soil moisture regularly and adjusting watering frequency accordingly.
  • Monitor Humidity Levels: Some indoor plants thrive in high humidity environments, so consider using a humidifier or pebble tray to increase humidity levels.
